## Our Standards

### Expected Behaviour

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the project and our clients.
- Show empathy and kindness toward other community members.
- Give credit generously and take responsibility for mistakes.

### Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Public or private harassment of any kind.
- Publishing others' private information (physical or electronic addresses) without explicit permission.
- Sexualised language, imagery, or unwelcome sexual attention.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.
